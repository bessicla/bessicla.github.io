Bessicla is Claudia Bessi,
an illustrator and multidisciplinary designer
based in Florence, Italy.

Claudia's work is influenced by pop art, food, fashion and tv series world, basically all that makes her eyes happy and her hand drawing eagerly. But if she need to make a list of her favorite source of inspiration she would definitely choose: Bruno Munari, Mary Blair, David Hockney, Wes Anderson, Agatha Ruiz De La Prada.

Big red cheeks and bold patterns define her illustrations, together with a talented use of colours.

Born in 1991 from an artistic family, her parents' studio became her playground, and drawing and visual art has always been her bread and butter.

After graduating from a design school in 2014, she started working as a creative director for the Italian online shop Family Nation, fostering her skills in UX and visual communication.

Now she works as freelancer Illustrator and Graphic Designer in a multidisciplinary studio together with other professionals of video making, copywriting, interior design and web design.

There is not a day that her sketch-book has not being filled by the colours and images that cross Claudia’s mind.

Selected clients:
